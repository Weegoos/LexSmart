**Project Topic:** LexSmart Kazakhstan: An automated legal compliance platform aimed at preventing labor disputes
**Project Type:** Website/web platform (www.lexsmart.kz)

The importance of digital technology in education and society. ## 2. Project Idea and Goal

Currently, the quality and legality of processing legal documents in the field of labor relations in the Republic of Kazakhstan is a pressing issue that requires special attention. In many organizations, especially in small and medium-sized business entities with limited financial capabilities, failure to fully comply with legislative requirements when drafting labor contracts is a frequent phenomenon. This problem is often directly related to the inaccessibility of professional legal assistance, a shortage of specialists, or the excessively high cost of legal services.

Technical or legal errors made in labor contracts and orders lead to conflicts between the employer and the employee, lengthy court proceedings, and heavy administrative fines imposed by state authorities. This causes not only financial losses but also a decline in the organization's business reputation in the market. In addition, the labor legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan is constantly changing and being supplemented. For entrepreneurs and HR specialists, monitoring these changes on a daily basis is a massive intellectual and physical burden. As a result, many institutions continue to use documents based on outdated normative acts, leaving themselves in a zone of legal risk.

To solve this systemic problem, the "LexSmart Kazakhstan" digital project is being proposed. The main idea of the project is to develop an intelligent web platform designed to fully automate the legal document flow in the field of labor relations and ensure their 100% compliance with the Labor Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The project will operate under the www.lexsmart.kz domain and will allow users to flawlessly generate labor contracts, supplementary agreements, and hiring or dismissal orders through an intuitive interface.

The main goal of the project is to ensure the legal security of subjects of labor relations and prevent labor disputes through innovative digital solutions. To achieve this goal, the following tasks are envisioned:

* 1. Reduce the time spent preparing legal documents by 80% through automation;
* 2. Track legislative changes in real-time and automatically update document templates;
* 3. Offer an accessible and affordable alternative for receiving legal advice for employers and employees.

Thus, the "LexSmart Kazakhstan" project not only simplifies the process of executing labor documents but also represents an important step towards forming a digital culture in the labor market and ensuring the transparency of the legal environment.

## 5. Website Description. Mechanism for solving legal issues using the website

*[Batyrbekuly Meirambek described the website mechanism and structure]*

The "LexSmart Kazakhstan" digital platform solves the main legal issue in the field of labor relations by automating the manual burden, errors, time and financial costs, as well as non-compliance with legislative requirements in the drafting, verification, management, and control of legal documents. The website executes the entire process online through an intuitive user interface.

* 1) Registration and profile creation. The user visits the site, registers, and enters the organization's parameters (type of organization, number of employees, industry specifics). This information serves as the basis for the system to correctly adapt the document templates.
* 2) Document constructor. The user goes to the "Create a new document" section and enters the necessary data through a step-by-step questionnaire: employee details, position, salary, work regime, probation period, vacation types, material liability, and other conditions. The system processes the entered data and automatically generates documents such as a labor contract, supplementary agreement, or order.
* 3) Automatic legal verification and risk identification. Once the document is ready, the system launches an analysis module: it checks for the presence of mandatory clauses, compliance with norms, and risky formulations. If a discrepancy is found, the system issues a warning and suggests a correction option. This mechanism serves to correct legal errors proactively and prevent disputes.
* 4) Automatic update. When a change is introduced to labor legislation, the platform updates the legal database and can assign a "needs updating" status to previous documents. The user quickly brings the documents into compliance with the new requirements. This reduces the need for manual reprocessing.
* 5) Signing, storage, and management. The finalized document is saved in an electronic format, and document histories and versions are systematized. The user can apply management functions such as searching, grouping, and monitoring notes.

As a result, procedures that traditionally take several hours are reduced to minutes, the probability of legal errors decreases, and the organization's compliance level increases.

## 6. Practical Value

The "LexSmart Kazakhstan" digital legal compliance platform is designed to automate legal processes in the field of labor relations and has broad applicability for various users. The application scenarios for the platform demonstrate its versatility and practical efficiency.

The first scenario is the drafting of labor contracts by employers. When an organization hires a new employee, the employer enters the employee's details, type of work, labor regime, and other necessary information into the platform. Based on this data, the system automatically generates a labor contract that fully complies with the labor legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan. This document can be downloaded ready-made in just a few minutes or saved electronically. This scenario is particularly effective for small and medium-sized businesses, as they might not have an in-house lawyer.

The second scenario is the legal verification of existing labor documents. An organization can use the platform to upload previously created labor contracts or supplementary agreements into the system and verify their compliance with legislative requirements. The platform identifies errors, missing clauses, or points that contradict the law within the document, and provides suggestions for their correction. This helps prevent labor disputes and reduces legal risks.

The third scenario is automating the daily work of the HR department. HR personnel can use the platform to automatically create documents such as hiring orders, dismissal orders, vacation processing, and changing an employee's position. This simplifies document flow and increases the efficiency of HR record-keeping.

The fourth scenario is staying informed about legislative changes. The platform automatically updates changes in the labor legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan and sends relevant notifications to users. This allows organizations to fulfill legal requirements on time and prevent legal violations.

The fifth scenario is providing document flow for remote-working organizations. By utilizing cloud technologies, users can access documents anytime and from anywhere. This is especially important for organizations with branches located in different cities.

The sixth scenario is application in the educational field. The platform can be used as a teaching tool in higher education institutions for students majoring in law, management, and human resources. Students practically acquire the skills of drafting labor contracts and observing legal requirements.

The seventh scenario is its use as a legal support tool for startups and newly established enterprises. By using the platform, new business entities can properly organize labor relations and prevent legal errors from the start.

Thus, the "LexSmart Kazakhstan" platform is a versatile digital tool that simplifies legal processes in the field of labor relations for employers, HR departments, entrepreneurs, educational organizations, and other users. The application scenarios for the platform prove its high practical significance and broad usability.

## 7. Project Implementation Plan

Technical tools, platform, programming

The implementation of the "LexSmart Kazakhstan" digital project will be carried out using modern web technologies, cloud infrastructure, and secure software solutions. The main goal of the project is to develop the www.lexsmart.kz web platform that is accessible, secure, and functions stably for users.

### 1. General Architecture of the Platform

The project will be developed in the form of a web platform and consists of three main levels:

* User interface (Frontend)
* Server part (Backend)
* Database

This architecture ensures the stable operation of the platform, secure storage of information, and high performance.

### 2. User Interface (Frontend)

The Frontend component provides interaction between the user and the system. This section will be developed based on the following technologies:

* HTML5 - creating web page structure
* CSS3 - design and visual styling
* JavaScript - implementing interactive functions
* React.js or Vue.js - creating a modern user interface

Main Frontend functions:

* User registration and authorization
* Form for creating labor contracts
* Uploading and saving documents
* User account interface displaying notifications and recommendations

### 3. Server Part (Backend)

The Backend implements the core logic of the platform and ensures data processing. The following programming languages and technologies are used for the Backend:

* Python (Django or Flask framework)
* or
* JavaScript (Node.js)

Backend functions:

* Processing user data
* Algorithm for automatically generating labor contracts
* Checking legal compliance
* Communication with the database
* Ensuring security

### 4. Database

In the platform, all legal documents, user data, and system information are stored in the database. Used as a database:

* PostgreSQL
* or
* MySQL

This system provides the following capabilities:

* Secure data storage
* Fast search
* Backups
* Maintaining data integrity

### 5. Cloud Technologies and Server

To ensure the platform operates stably on the internet, it is planned to be hosted on a cloud server. Cloud platforms to be used:

* Amazon Web Services (AWS)

This provides the following features:

* 24/7 availability

To protect user data on the platform, the following security technologies will be used:
